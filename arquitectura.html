ARQUITECTURA DE COMPUTADORAS
That is?

Is a fundamental digital circuit in a computer processor. It is responsible for
performing arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
and logical operations (AND, OR, NOT, XOR) between two or more data values.



What it is for?

ALUs have a wide range of applications in the world of computing. Some of the most important are:

Executing arithmetic instructions
Perform logical operations
Implementing algorithms
Data processing
Execute code
